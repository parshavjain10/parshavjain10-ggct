Department Of Humanities

About Faculty Vision Salient Features

The language laboratory is an audio or audio-visual installation used as an aid in modern language teaching. The language department is established in 2007. There are different features of language lab, which make the students have an interactive session. It provides equal opportunity to all the students to hear the instructor irrespective of the place where they are seated. There will be less miscommunication because of direct nature of the sound transmission. It also provides the privacy that encourages the shy students to speak without any hesitation. In addition,an instructor can speak to individual or group of students in privacy without interrupting rest of class. Language labs motivate students to talk freely and lose the shyness when talking in front of their friends. Attention on the subject is increased resulting in better retention of the concepts.
